# Source: Notes

Your own reading notes, scratch thinking, and observations that aren't formal analyses.

## Naming

`YYYY-MM-DD-short-slug.md` — e.g. `2026-06-02-thinking-through-pairing-flow.md`

## What goes here

- Notes you took while reading docs or watching a talk
- Stream-of-thought processing of a problem
- Diagrams or sketches as code/mermaid
- Open questions you want to come back to

The distinction from `analyses/`: notes are exploratory and personal, analyses are structured and decision-oriented.
